My Life at JU

I come to jaipur on 30th june 2016.. from that day this is the turning point of my life. This University is very good for academics and extracurricular activities. This college give a exposure for buildup our personality, skills. I really enjoy the environment here. The knowledge and teaching skills of the faculty are highly impressive. The eco-friendly and pleasant environment adds to the appeal of campus. JECRC not only provides a world- class environment for academic curriculum but also a bouquet of opportunities to enhance your inter-personal, analytical, managerial and leadership skills. There is noboundary or wall between the students and faculty and we work as a family to strive for excellence. I am honored to be a part of such renowned and fine institute.

Living in Campus

On-campus residential facility for students has been very thoughtfully designed and planned. Each unit in the JU Hostel Complex allows privacy and personal space to a student while giving a chance to dwell together and foster comradeship. An apartment style -three private, single occupancy rooms open into a common living room equipped with a television set, internet access and a pantry making it a complete independent unit. The hostels are air-conditioned, comfortable and conducive for the students to have a productive campus life, providing a perfect setting for academic pursuits.

Students Clubs & other Activities

Students are encouraged to participate in co-curricular and extracurricular activities through these clubs and societies.
